Production Growth

Foodgrain production continues to grow despite fluctuating yields in some crops, showing resilience in India's agricultural sector.

Pulses Concern

Pulses production shows a concerning decline due to shrinking cultivated area, requiring immediate attention and policy intervention.

Commercial Crops

Sugarcane and cotton maintain strong production levels, though sugarcane yield has fallen, indicating need for yield improvement strategies.

Staple Performance

Oilseeds remain stable, while rice and wheat continue to perform as staple crops with consistent gains in production.

MSP Returns

Most crops offer 50%+ returns over cost, with wheat and rapeseed showing exceptional returns of 100%+, ensuring farmer profitability.

Market Support

Active market intervention schemes provide price support during market distress, protecting farmer interests across various crops and regions.
