What is the required Nutrients for agricultural land?

" Macro Nutrients: Nitrogen, Potassium, Magnesium,Sulphur, Phosphorus,Calcium Micro Nutrients: Iron,Zinc,Copper, Manganese, Chlorine, Molybdenum,Boron."

What are the required soil properties for Agricultural Land?

"The pH value of land should be within the range of 6.0-7.5. The land must contain good texture, water holding, aeration and many other. Land must contain Rich in organic material and microbes. "

What are the disadvantages of using pesticides in fertile land?

"It kills beneficial microbes,earthworms and it reduces fertility. Pests develop resistance are needed for stronger chemicals. It causes nutrient imbalance in soil. It pollute water sources,harm beeds, birds and biodiversity. It leave harmful residues and leads to health risks. It leads to long term soil degradarion. "
